  • Chicken Curry Recipe
Marination Time : 20 mins to 1 hr
Preparation Time : 35 mins
Serves : 4 People

  • INGREDIENTS TO MAKE THE RECIPE

    * 3 Mid – Size Onions (200gms)

    * 3 Mid – Size Tomatoes (200gms)

    * 600-800 gms Chicken Marinated

    * 2 Tbsp Ghee

    * 1/4th Cup Oil

    * ½ tsp Turmeric Powder

    * 2 tsp Salt

    * 1.5 Tbsp Kasuri Methi

    * ½ tsp Garam Masala

    * 2 Cups Water

    * 1 Tbsp Ginger & Garlic Paste

    ** Ground Spice Mix

    ** Whole Spice Mix

  • INGREDIENTS TO MAKE GROUND SPICE MIX

    * 2.5 tsp Coriander Powder

    * ½ tsp Jeera Powder

    * 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder

    * 2 tsp Roasted Gram Flour (Besan Or Chicpea)

  • INGREDIENTS TO MAKE WHOLE SPICE MIX

    * 4-5 Black Peppercorns / Kali Mirch

    * 4-5 Cloves/Laung

    * 2 Green Cardamom / Choti Elachi

    * 2 Bay Leafs/Tej Patta

    * 1 Black Cardamom / Badi Elachi

    * 1 Cinnamon Stick/ Dal Chini

    * 2 Dried Red Chillies

  • INGREDIENTS TO MARINATE THE CHICKEN

    * 1 Tbsp Ginger & Garlic Paste

    * 1 tsp Salt

    * 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder

    * 2 Tbsp Lemon Juice or 1 Tbsp Vinegar

  • Steps To Make Chicken Curry

    MARINATING THE CHICKEN

    Apply 1 Tbsp Ginger & Garlic Paste,1 tsp Salt,1 tsp Red Chilli Powder, 2 Tbsp Lemon Juice or 1 Tbsp Vinegar, And keep it to marinate for 20 mins to 1 hr.

    Step 1:

    1. Take a Pot and First dry roast all the whole spices on a low flame (195 F) for about 2 minutes.

    2. Add 2Tbsp Ghee and wait for the spices to sizzle.

    3. Now add 1/4 cup oil which will suddenly drop the temperature.

    Step 2:

    1. As oil and ghee tempereature rises, now add finely Chopped onions and as the onions get 80% cooked add 1Tbsp ginger garlic paste to make it golden brown.

    2. At this point add the strained curd and add 1/2 tsp turmeric powder while keeping the gas on low flame (195 F) and cook for 10 minutes.

    3. At this point you will see the oil seprating from masala, now add tomato and salt to taste and cook for 5 minutes.

    Step 3:

    1. Now add ground spice mix and cook for 2-3 minutes.

    2. Turn the flame to high (400 F) and add the marinated chicken and cook it for 3 - 4 minutes until it change its colour from pink to white, now turn the flame low (195 F) and add 2 cups of water and cover the pot with a lid.

    Step 4:

    1. Let it cook for 20 minutes, On high flame (400 F).

    * Add some kasuri methi, garam masala and butter mix it well.

    * Now, Chicken is ready to be served.
